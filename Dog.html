Step 1

You will first need to go and find any sort of dog treat that will get your dog's attention.
Something like this doggy bone I have will do.

Step 2

Once you have your doggy treat, get your dog's attention by showing that you have a yummy treat.

As you can see, I definitely have her attention now by showing her what's in my hand.

Step 3

Okay, now here is the important part.
You need to let your dog earn the treat. You cannot give them the treat because they want it solely. Tell them to do a trick or to walk around with you.
Watch below for a demonstration.

Look at her! Such a good dog!

Step 4

Now your dog should have some energy and will be excited for the next treat!
As you can see, my dog just eats on the ground and will go back to being lazy. I guess I'm not the best teacher at showing how to do this after all...
